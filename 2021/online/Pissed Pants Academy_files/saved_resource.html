A publishing platform to reimagine established relationships between reader, text, and author, and to fully utilize the affordances the web offers in the presentation of text.

Contributors are asked to submit texts that might lend themselves well to non-linear readings, which are then re-interpreted into their own websites.

Designed, edited, and developed by Tiger Dingsun. Interested in contributing? Inquire at tiger.dingsun@gmail.com.
